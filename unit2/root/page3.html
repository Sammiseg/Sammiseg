Training the Pitbull

Like any other dog training and socializing is key. Due to the stubborn nature and how powerful an animal is they are not reccomended for first time dog owners or ones that aren't familiar with the breed. On top of that elderly and children would not be wise to take care of these dogs due to the power/muscle they posses.

Pro's to Training your dog

  1. Well behaved dog
  2. Less likely to have behavior issues
  3. Can help with Keeping the dog out of trouble
